NCERT Solutions for Class 6 English Chapter 1 Poem - A House, A Home

Working with the poem (Page 16)

Answer the following questions given below:

Note: We are providing four alternate answers of each questions so don't be confused. Also, "★" refers to our recommended answer out of the four.

1. Do you agree with what the poet says? Talk to your partner and complete these sentences.

(i) A house is made of _______

Answer: A house is made of brick, stone, cement, wood which are lifeless.

(ii) It has _____

Answer: It has many windows, many doors, roof, floor, chimneys, eaves etc.

(iii) A home is made by _______

Answer: A home is made by a loving and caring family and family members.

(iv) It has _______

Answer: It has members who have love and care for each other without beign selfish.

2. Now complete these sentences about your house and home.

(i) My house is _______

Answer:My house is made up of bricks, marbles and tiles. It has beautiful windows made up of glass with a balcony and a roof.

or

Answer: My house is very big. My house has bedrooms, bathrooms, kitchen, prayer room and dining room. I love my house so much.

or

Answer: My house is built of bricks and cement. It has nice doors and windows. It also consists of beautiful floors, a roof, and a lovely garden.

or

Answer: My house is built up of bricks, floor tiles and a beautiful yard with many small plants. It has many windows and doors. It also has a terrace. The living area is too spacious with beautiful curtains all around.

(ii) The best thing about my home is _______

Answer: The best thing about my home is that all of us understand and help each other in any distress. We live together peacefully and take out time for each other. We care for each other.

or

Answer: The best thing about my home is homes offer security and feel affection for human life and it is one of the most important things.

or

Answer: The best thing about my home is that it is a sweet home because my parents are very much loving and caring.

or

Answer:The best thing about my home is I have grandparents who are very loving and caring. My parents are also very loving and caring, we all play games together. My grandmother reads bedtime stories for me every day. I play with my grandfather every day in the morning. We have our breakfast, lunch and dinner together.
